Projects and interests!

During my masters and till of PhD. I have been busy doing several projects.They were never aligned to a single topic but I could learn a ton of new things out of them.

Growth of Gas Hydrate

A project to study and compare the growth of methane hydrates in presence of common alcohols such as ethanol, glycerol, isopropyl alcohol and ethylene glycol and 3 different osmolytes urea, DMSO and TMAO. We tried to hypothesize the behaviour of growth of gas hydrate around additives using various order paramter.

Silver nanoparticle nucleation

During my masters' I started this project where I had to observe the nucleation event and explain the event in terms of two step nucleation method. We observed that there is a spontaneous hcp to fcc transition during nucleation. This is an unfinished project.

Project Liquid Crystal

I had this project as a side quest. Learned a lot of new things during this project as I was completely off guard to This kind of systems.





Project Free Energy of Solvation

Another unfinished yet interesting project it was. It is important to understand the efect of solvent (water) as most of the reaction occurs in solvated condition. But explicit consideration of solvent-solute intraction is costly in computational term. So one's wish would be to introduce accurate yet efficient implicit solvent model. That was the long term philosophy of this project.



ML/DL

We are trying to introduce Machiene learning and Deep learning methods to solve our problems. Our primary focus has been to be able to use efficient ML or DL model to detect phase transition. We implemented PointNet architect to classify different ice phases. We are very enthusiastic about these methods and have been trying various algorithm to improve abd benchmark available models. In long run we wish to made our own archtect to predict chemico-moleculalar properties of arbitrary molecules.
